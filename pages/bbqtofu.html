Barbeque Tofu

Description

Are you looking for an easy and flavorful weeknight dish?

Look no further than this beginner-friendly guide to barbeque tofu.

Using simple kitchen ingredients, this evening meal whips itself together in under an hour. It's sure to please your children, spouses, and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 14-ounce block of extra firm tofu (pressed for 30 minutes and cut into slabs)
  • Barbeque sauce (for brushing)
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place the tofu on a plate and brush with BBQ sauce on all sides. Arrange in a single layer on the baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes, or until browned around the edges. Flip, brush with more BBQ sauce, and bake for 5 minutes, or until well browned. Serve with more BBQ sauce, if desired.
